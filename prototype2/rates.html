Policies:

  • A security deposit ($150 for log cabins) is required to secure a reservation. This can be done with a personal check or credit card.

  • In the event of the need to cancel a reservation, the deposit is refundable (less a $25 fee) if the cancellation is done 4 weeks prior to the stay.

  • The Moose Lodge cabin is a non-refundable reservation and must be paid for in advance. If multiple cabins are rented, the reservation is non-refundable and must be paid in advance. Otherwise, payment for the stay is required upon arrival.

  • The security deposit is refunded at check-out (or within 2 weeks of departure if check-out is not performed).

  • Linens are not provided at the above prices. Linens/Blankets are available at a cost of $10.00/set.

  • The above prices do not include our local tax.

  • For the comfort of all, Pleasant Valley does not permit inside smoking.

  • We do permit pets in our Bird's Nest Cabin Only (with prior arrangement). There is a $15 pet fee per 2 night stay and $5 per additional night.
